SECURITY
The Vigipirate Plan – Reinforced Security in
effect in France requires control measures.
Please anticipate possible delays when
accessing the theaters.
Scissors, pocket knives, motorcycle helmets,
cans, and similar items are prohibited.

RULES

on the Red Carpet and inside the theaters. Any
violation will result in denied access.
