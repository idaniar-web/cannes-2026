How to find the projection ID
  1. Open the booking site and go to the screening you want.
  2. Look at the URL in your browser. It ends with ?idproj=….
  3. Copy everything after ?idproj=.
Example URL:
https://ticketonline2.festival-cannes.com/fiche?idproj=Jfu6YKcXhM4%3D
Projection ID = Jfu6YKcXhM4= (the %3D is just =; either form works).
What's "allow swap"? If a ticket becomes available but you already hold a ticket for an overlapping screening, ticking this will release your existing ticket so the new one can be booked. Untick to skip booking when there's a conflict.
